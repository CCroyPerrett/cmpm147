Experiment 4 - Infinite Worlds

Description

With this experiment, I sought out to create three different tile based world generators, utilizing p5.js's tileset systeming to create animated worlds with depth. Images below display possible outputs of the generated worlds presented.

Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works? This generator inserts life into a watery expanse of fishing boats, in the middle of the open ocean. To create a sense of life, fish are spawned across the water, of varying sizes and rotation to represent swimming in place. Boats also frequent the scene, bobbing up and down the water.
