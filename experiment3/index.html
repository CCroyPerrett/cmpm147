Experiment 3 - Living World

Description

With this experiment, I sought out to create two random generators, one to design a dungeon like environment, and one to give an impression of a breathing world, utilizing a tiling system./p>

Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works? Above can be seen two example images of my world generator, and the links to the generators can be found below. The World design generator is done iterativley, first generating the ground (including darker partches), and then overlaying the rivers, houses & trees in random patterns around such. To make the world feel more alive, the grass has an animated effect (which I am quite proud to have discovered how to impliment on accident, while trying to do something else), & the houses Light up and darked periodically, to represent the flicker of torchlight. Should the environment have the "snowy tag" selected, than decor on the scene will utilize their snowy counterparts.
