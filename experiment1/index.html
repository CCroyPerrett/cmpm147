Experiment 1 - Getting Started

Description

For this experiment, I set out to create a simple story generator. Through this finished product, one an generate a short & sweet murder mystery in a matter of seconds, asking the player if they can utilize the provided information to find a killer. There are near limitless combinations! Can you solve the mystery and claim your reward?

Technical

This generator takes a predefined promt, and randomly inserts words from a hand-created set to build different murder senarios. For axample, there is a list for the suspects' names, the victm's name, the murder location, etc. Some lists are utilized several times (to generate a number of possible suspects), which does lead to a slight risk of repeat names, so certain lists were extended to a great length to make that less likley. Through utilizing Jquery, a short and consise mystery can be created.

Reflection

Although I am happy with what I have created so far, this assignmetn only drives me to want to create bigger and better generators. Although what I have created is sufficient, and at times humorous, it is rather restricted in scope. And allthough there isa solvable mystery (with clues that make sense), a majority of the time, occasionally what is presented does not lead to much but dead investigative ends (the randomly selected clues may sometimes not poitn to any of the randomly selected suspects).
